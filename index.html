AI Engine
Built for speed, built for trust
Every complaint analyzed, classified, and prioritized in under 2 seconds.
🧠
NLP Classification
Fine-tuned BERT model classifies into 12 civic categories with 96% accuracy — Water, Roads, Safety, Health, and more.
😤
Sentiment Detection
3-class emotion engine: Normal, Angry, Urgent. Urgent complaints trigger immediate escalation within 15 minutes.
🎯
Priority Scoring
Weighted formula: P = (Urgency×0.4)+(Impact×0.3)+(Location×0.2)+(Time×0.1). Score 1–10 in real time.
🔗
Duplicate Detection
TF-IDF cosine similarity merges duplicate complaints. Community votes amplify priority automatically.
🔮
Predictive Analytics
Time-series forecasting flags complaint hotspots 7–14 days ahead for proactive department action.
⚡
Auto-Routing
ML routing engine assigns the right department. SLA clock starts automatically. Escalation on breach.
